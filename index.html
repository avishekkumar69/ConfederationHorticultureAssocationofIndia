Our Mission

The Confederation Horticulture Association of India (CHAI) acts as a unified voice for horticulturists, researchers, and policy makers. Established with a vision to make India a global leader in horticulture, we focus on:

  • Enhancing productivity and quality of produce.
  • Post-harvest management and reduction of wastage.
  • Organizing national and international conferences.
